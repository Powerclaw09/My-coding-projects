About Us

Tech Code Simple is a fun, interactive virtual space where kids aged 5-11 explore technology through engaging 1-on-1 or group classes. With a wide range of tech topics to choose from, we empower other children to learn and create with confidence.

Backstory

One day, as I was watching YouTube and relaxing, an idea sparked: what if my small “tech club” could become something bigger? Originally, I ran this club for my classmates, giving them a space to learn about technology in fun, one-hour sessions. But that day, I envisioned transforming it into a company—a global space where kids everywhere could learn technology affordably. And that’s how Tech Code Simple was born.
